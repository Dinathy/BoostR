About Us

We Are BoostR

Founded in Sri Lanka, boostR is a passionate and innovative social media marketing startup dedicated to helping businesses and startups amplify their digital presence. With a strong focus on creativity, data-driven strategies, and client collaboration, we offer personalized solutions to accelerate growth across social platforms. Our team is committed to delivering measurable results through targeted campaigns, engaging content, and continuous optimization to meet the ever-evolving needs of today’s digital landscape.

Why Choose Us

Tailored Strategies.

We believe that no two businesses are the same. That’s why we develop personalized social media marketing strategies that align with your specific goals, industry, and target audience. Every campaign is crafted to meet your unique needs, ensuring maximum impact and relevance.

Client-Centred Approach.

We prioritize close collaboration with our clients. Your vision and business objectives are at the core of everything we do. We take the time to understand your brand’s values and goals, ensuring every social media strategy we build truly reflects your business identity.

Adaptability & Relavance.

Social media is constantly evolving, and so are we. Our team stays on top of the latest industry trends, platform updates, and digital marketing innovations to keep your campaigns fresh and competitive. We adapt to changes in algorithms, consumer behavior, and market conditions to maintain momentum.

Transparency & Trust.

We believe in clear communication and full transparency with our clients. You’ll always know what’s happening with your campaigns, what’s working, and what can be improved. With Boostr, you can trust that we’re as dedicated to your success as you are.
